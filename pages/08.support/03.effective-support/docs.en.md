We strive to offer the best support experience in our market. This means we aim to provide you with fast, knowledgeable, and accurate assistance every day (and night) of the year.

# Opening tickets
The quality of our support also depends on your collaboration. In particular please:

* Try to keep one issue per ticket
* Provide the server hostname or IP address
* Provide the full URL
* Provide a detailed description of the issue you are encountering.
* Don't resume old tickets. Open a new ticket and reference the old ticket mask

# Priority escalations
We aim to handle all issues as promptly as possible, but if your issue is business critical and you did not receive an update within 15 minutes please feel free to email the address indicated in the initial reply. Make sure the ticket mask is included in the subject line for the ticket to be prioritised by our support team.

! Kindly note that priority escalations within the first 15 minutes of ticket creation will be rejected.

# Feedback
Our management team constantly tune our internal support processes to maintain support quality. Feedback regarding how we can serve your needs better, praise for a specific engineer that saved your day, or concerns requiring management review of any given ticket, may be shared to the management email address in our ticket auto-responder.
